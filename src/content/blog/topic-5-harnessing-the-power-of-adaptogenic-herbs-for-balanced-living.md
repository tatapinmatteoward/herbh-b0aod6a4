## What Are Adaptogens?

Adaptogens are a unique class of herbs and plants that help regulate the body's stress response. Unlike stimulants that provide a quick but fleeting boost, adaptogens work gently over time to support your adrenal glands, balance hormones, and enhance overall resilience. At Herb & Harmony, we source premium adaptogens like ashwagandha, rhodiola, and holy basil to craft blends that fit seamlessly into your daily routine.

## The Science-Backed Benefits

Research shows adaptogens can:
- **Reduce Stress and Anxiety**: By modulating cortisol levels, they promote a calm state without drowsiness.
- **Boost Energy and Focus**: They enhance mental clarity and stamina, ideal for busy professionals.
- **Support Immune Function**: Their antioxidant properties fortify your defenses against daily wear and tear.
- **Improve Sleep Quality**: Many adaptogens, like our Harmony Night blend, ease you into restorative rest.
