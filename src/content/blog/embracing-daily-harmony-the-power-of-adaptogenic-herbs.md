## What Are Adaptogens?

Adaptogens are a unique class of herbs that support the body's natural ability to handle stress. Unlike stimulants that provide a quick but temporary boost, adaptogens work gently over time to regulate cortisol levels, boost energy, and enhance resilience. Popular examples include ashwagandha, rhodiola, and holy basil, all featured in our premium blends.

### Key Benefits for Your Daily Routine

- **Immune Support**: Holy basil, or tulsi, strengthens defenses while fostering emotional balance.
